# Research Notes

## Key Problems in Current LLM Systems

### 1. Limited Context Window
Most LLMs struggle to maintain long-term contextual awareness.

### 2. Hallucinations
LLMs may generate incorrect information without external grounding.

### 3. Lack of Persistent Memory
Traditional systems forget previous interactions after session completion.

### 4. Weak Personalization
Current AI systems often fail to adapt to user-specific behavior patterns.

### 5. Inconsistent Reasoning
Reasoning quality degrades over extended conversations.

---

# Why Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)?

RAG improves AI systems by retrieving relevant external information before generating responses.

Benefits:
- Improved factual accuracy
- Reduced hallucinations
- Better contextual grounding
- Dynamic knowledge retrieval
- Enhanced explainability
